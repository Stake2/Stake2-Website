The Secret of the Crystals:

Synopsis: "This is the story of Ana, a fairy that lives on the planet Earth, a crystal version, where several different species live.
She lives on the Crystal Lands, where the fairies live, where many crystals come out of the ground.
Other species live on this planet too, besides fairies.
Ana and the fairies do not know the origin of the crystals, where they came from, or how they formed.
She is an adventurous and brave girl, she loves exploring her planet, flying, and discovering new islands.
Ana will go in search of the secret of the crystals to try to unravel this mystery that has been around for decades.
She also does not know where the fairies and other creatures came from, or what was the past of the world.
The girl does not know what is beyond the sky, the stars, she wants to discover that.
And this is the story of her journey, what will be waiting for Ana in this journey? New Friends? New places?
We will have to read to know, here begins The Secret of the Crystals."
